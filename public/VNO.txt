Vornado's portfolio is concentrated in the nation's key market  New York City
along with the premier asset in both Chicago and San Francisco. Vornado is also
the real estate industry leader in sustainability policy. The company owns and
manages over 23 million square feet of LEED certified buildings and received
the Energy Star Partner of the Year Award, Sustained Excellence 2019. In 2012,
Vornado commemorated 50 years on the NYSE.
