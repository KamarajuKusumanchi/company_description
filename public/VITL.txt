Vital Farms, Inc. packages, markets and distributes pasture-raised shell eggs,
pasture-raised butter and other products. Its products are sold under the trade
names Vital Farms, Alfresco Farms, Lucky Ladies and RedHill Farms, primarily to
retail foodservice channels. The company was founded by Catherine Stewart,
Matthew O'Hayer and Jason Jones in 2007 and is headquartered in Austin, TX.
