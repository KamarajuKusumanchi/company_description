The Trade Desk, Inc. engages in the provision of a self-service and cloud-based
ad-buying platform. It operates through the United States and International
geographical segments. The firm offers omnichannel advertising, audience
targeting, solutions for identity, application programming interface (API),
custom, and programmatic, measurement and optimization. The company was founded
by Jeffrey Terry Green and David Pickles in November 2009 and is headquartered
in Ventura, CA.
