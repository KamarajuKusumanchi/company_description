Constellation Energy Corp. engages in the generation, supply, and marketing of
clean electricity, and renewable energy products and solutions. The firm also
offers wholesale energy, retail products and services. It operates under the
following geographical segments: Mid-Atlantic, Midwest, New York, Electric
Reliability Council of Texas (ERCOT), and Other Power Regions Segment. The
company was founded in 1960 and is headquartered in Baltimore, MD.
