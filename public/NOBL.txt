ProShares S&P 500 Dividend Aristocrats ETF seeks investment results, before
fees and expenses, that track the performance of the S&P 500 Dividend
Aristocrats Index (the "index"). The fund will invest at least 80% of its total
assets in component securities of the index. The index contains a minimum of 40
stocks, which are equally weighted, and no single sector is allowed to comprise
more than 30% of the index weight. It seeks to remain fully invested at all
times in securities and/or financial instruments that, in combination, provide
exposure to the returns of the index without regard to market conditions,
trends or direction.
