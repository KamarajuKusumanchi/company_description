On Holding AG engages in the development and distribution of sports products
such as footwear, apparel, and accessories for high-performance running,
outdoor, and all-day activities. It sells its products worldwide through
independent retailers and global distributors, its own online presence, and its
own high-end stores. The company was founded by David Allemann, Olivier
Bernhard, and Caspar Coppetti in January 2010 and is headquartered in Zurich,
Switzerland.
