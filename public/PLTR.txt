Palantir Technologies, Inc. engages in the business of building and deploying
software platforms that serve as the central operating systems for its
customers. It operates under the Commercial and Government segments. The
Commercial segment focuses on customers working in non-government industries.
The Government segment is involved in providing services to customers that are
the United States government and non-United States government agencies. The
company was founded by Alexander Ceadmon Karp, Peter Andreas Thiel, Stephen
Cohen, and Nathan Dale Gettings in 2003 and is headquartered in Denver, CO.
