The Kraft Heinz Co. engages in the manufacture and market of food and beverage
products. The firm operates through the following geographical segments: United
States, Canada and International. Its brands include Oscar Meyer, Velveeta,
Smart Ones, Caprisun, Kool-Aid, Golden circle and Honig. The company was
founded on July 2, 2015, and is headquartered in Pittsburgh, PA.
