XPeng Inc. designs, develops, manufactures, and markets smart electric vehicles
in the People's Republic of China. It offers SUVs under the G3 and G3i names;
four-door sports sedans under the P7 name; and family sedans under the P5 name.
The company also provides sales contracts, maintenance, super charging, vehicle
leasing, insurance agency, ride-hailing, technical support, automotive loan
referral and auto financing, music subscription, and other services. XPeng Inc.
was founded in 2015 and is headquartered in Guangzhou, the People's Republic of
China.
