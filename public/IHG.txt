InterContinental Hotels Group Plc is a global hospitality company. The firm's
hotel brands include Six Senses, Regent, Intercontinental, Vignette, Kimpton,
Hotel Indigo, Voco, Hualuxe, Crowne Plaza, Iberostar, Even, Holiday Inn
Express, Holiday Inn, Garner, Avid, Atwell Suites, Staybridge Suites, Holiday
Inn Club Vacations, Candlewood, and IHG One Rewards. It operates through the
following segments: Europe, Middle East, Asia and Africa, Americas, Greater
China, and Central. The company was founded in 1777 and is headquartered in
Denham, the United Kingdom.
