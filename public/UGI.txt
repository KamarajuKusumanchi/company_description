UGI Corp. is a holding company, which engages in the distribution,
transportation, storing, and marketing of energy products and services. It
operates through the following segments: AmeriGas Propane, UGI International,
Midstream and Marketing, and Utilities. The AmeriGas Propane segment consists
of the propane distribution business of AmeriGas Partners. The UGI
International segment focuses on LPG distribution businesses conducted by its
subsidiaries and affiliates. The Midstream and Marketing segment is involved in
the energy-related businesses through its wholly owned subsidiary, Energy
Services. The Utilities segment includes the natural gas and electricity
distribution businesses directly and through its wholly owned subsidiaries. The
company was founded on June 1, 1882, and is headquartered in King of Prussia,
PA.
