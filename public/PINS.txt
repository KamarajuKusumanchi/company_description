Pinterest, Inc. operates as a visual discovery engine in the United States and
internationally. The company's engine allows people to find inspiration for
their lives, including recipes, style and home inspiration, DIY, and others;
and provides video, product, and idea pins. It shows visual machine learning
recommendations based on pinners taste and interests. The company was formerly
known as Cold Brew Labs Inc. and changed its name to Pinterest, Inc. in April
2012. Pinterest, Inc. was incorporated in 2008 and is headquartered in San
Francisco, California.
