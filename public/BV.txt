BrightView Holdings, Inc. is an investment company, which engages in the
provision of commercial landscaping services. It operates through the
Maintenance Services and Development Services segments. The Maintenance
Services segment focuses on recurring commercial landscaping services ranging
from mowing, gardening, mulching and snow removal, to more horticulturally
advanced services, such as water management, irrigation maintenance, tree care,
golf course maintenance, and specialty turf maintenance. The Development
Services segment offers landscape architecture and development services for new
facilities and significant redesign projects. The company was founded on
November 7, 2013 and is headquartered in Blue Bell, PA.
