Johnson & Johnson is a holding company, which engages in the research,
development, manufacture, and sale of products in the healthcare field. It
operates through the Innovative Medicine and MedTech segments. The Innovative
Medicine segment focuses on immunology, infectious diseases, neuroscience,
oncology, cardiovascular and metabolism, and pulmonary hypertension. The
MedTech segment includes a portfolio of products used in the interventional
solutions, orthopaedics, surgery, and vision categories. The company was
founded by Robert Wood Johnson I, James Wood Johnson, and Edward Mead Johnson
Sr. in 1887 and is headquartered in New Brunswick, NJ.
