Paychex, Inc. engages in the provision of human capital management solutions
for payroll, human resources, insurance and retirement for small and medium-
sized businesses. Its solutions include payroll services, hiring services,
business insurance, time and attendance, employee benefits, finance and
payments, human resources services and startup services. The company was
founded by Blase Thomas Golisano in 1971 and is headquartered in Rochester, NY.
