Utilities Select Sector SPDR Fund seeks to provide investment results that,
before expenses, correspond generally to the price and yield performance of
publicly traded equity securities of companies in the Utilities Select Sector
Index. In seeking to track the performance of the index, the fund employs a
replication strategy. It generally invests substantially all, but at least 95%,
of its total assets in the securities comprising the index. The index includes
securities of companies from the following industries: electric utilities;
water utilities; multi-utilities; independent power and renewable electricity
producers; and gas utilities. The fund is non-diversified.
