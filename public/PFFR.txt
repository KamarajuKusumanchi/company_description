InfraCap REIT Preferred ETF seeks investment results that correspond, before
fees and expenses, to the price and yield performance of the Indxx REIT
Preferred Stock Index (the "underlying index"). Under normal market conditions,
the fund will invest not less than 90% of its assets in component securities of
the underlying index. The underlying index is comprised of preferred securities
listed on U.S. exchanges that are issued by real estate investment trusts
("REITs"). The fund is non-diversified.
