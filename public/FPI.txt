Farmland Partners Inc. is an internally managed real estate company that owns
and seeks to acquire high-quality North American farmland and makes loans to
farmers secured by farm real estate. As of the date of this release, the
Company owns approximately 155,000 acres in 16 states, including Alabama,
Arkansas, California, Colorado, Florida, Georgia, Illinois, Kansas, Louisiana,
Michigan, Mississippi, Nebraska, North Carolina, South Carolina, South Dakota
and Virginia. We have approximately 26 crop types and over 100 tenants. The
Company elected to be taxed as a real estate investment trust, or REIT, for
U.S. federal income tax purposes, commencing with the taxable year ended
December 31, 2014.
