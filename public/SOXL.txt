Direxion Daily Semiconductor Bull 3X Shares seeks daily investment results,
before fees and expenses, of 300% of the daily performance of the ICE
Semiconductor Index. The fund invests at least 80% of its net assets (plus
borrowing for investment purposes) in financial instruments, such as swap
agreements, securities of the index, and ETFs that track the index and other
financial instruments that provide daily leveraged exposure to the index or to
ETFs that track the index. The index is a rules-based, modified float-adjusted
market capitalization-weighted index that tracks the performance of the thirty
largest U.S. listed semiconductor companies. The fund is non-diversified.
