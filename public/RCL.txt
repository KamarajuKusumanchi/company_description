Royal Caribbean Cruises Ltd. operates as a cruise company worldwide. The
company operates cruises under the Royal Caribbean International, Celebrity
Cruises, Azamara, and Silversea Cruises brands, which comprise a range of
itineraries that call on approximately 1,000 destinations. As of February 25,
2022, it operated 61 ships. The company was founded in 1968 and is
headquartered in Miami, Florida.
