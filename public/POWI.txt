Power Integrations, Inc. designs and develops mixed-signal integrated circuits
and other electronic components and circuitry used in high-voltage power
conversion. Its products include AC-DC Conversion, LED Drivers, Gate Drivers,
Motor Drivers, and Automotive Solutions. The company was founded by Klas
Eklund, Art Fury and Steven J. Sharp on March 25, 1988 and is headquartered in
San Jose, CA.
