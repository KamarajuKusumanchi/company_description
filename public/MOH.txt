Molina Healthcare, Inc. provides managed health care services to low-income
families and individuals under the Medicaid and Medicare programs and through
the state insurance marketplaces. It operates in four segments, Medicaid,
Medicare, Marketplace, and Other. As of December 31, 2021, the company served
the company served approximately 5.2 million members eligible for Medicaid,
Medicare, and other government-sponsored healthcare programs in 18 states. The
company was founded in 1980 and is headquartered in Long Beach, California.
