Progress Software Corp. engages in the provision of products that develop and
deploy mission-critical business applications. Its products and solutions
include OpenEdge, Developer Tools, Sitefinity, Corticon, DataDirect Connect,
MOVEit, Chef, WhatsUp Gold, Kemp Loadmaster, and Kemp Flowmon Network
Visibility. The company was founded by Joseph Wright Alsop, Clyde Kessel, and
Charles Arthur Ziering in 1981 and is headquartered in Burlington, MA.
