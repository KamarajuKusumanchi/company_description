Mondelez International, Inc. engages in the manufacture and marketing of snack
food and beverage products. It operates through the following geographical
segments: Latin America, Asia, Middle East, and Africa, Europe, and North
America. Its products include beverages, biscuits, chocolate, gum and candy,
cheese and grocery, and meals. Its brands include, but are not limited to,
Nabisco, Oreo, and LU biscuits, Cadbury, Cadbury Dairy Milk, and Milka
chocolates, and Trident gum. The company was founded by James Lewis Kraft in
1903 is headquartered in Chicago, IL.
