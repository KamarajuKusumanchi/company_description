Mattel, Inc. engages in the ownership of children's and family entertainment
franchises. It operates through the following segments: North America,
International, and American Girl. The North America and International segments
focus on marketing and selling toys and consumer products. The American Girl
segment includes marketing, retailing, and publishing dedicated to its mission
to help girls grow up with confidence and character. The company was founded by
Elliot Handler, Ruth Handler, and Harold Matson in 1945 and is headquartered in
El Segundo, CA.
