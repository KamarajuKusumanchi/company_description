Kellanova engages in the manufacturing, marketing, and distribution of ready-
to-eat cereal and convenience foods. It operates through the following
segments: North America, Europe, Latin America, and Asia Middle East Africa.
The North America segment includes the U.S. businesses and Canada. The Europe
segment consists of European countries. The Latin America segment focuses on
Central and South America, and Mexico. The Asia Middle East Africa segment is
involved in Africa, Middle East, Australia, and Other Asian and Pacific
markets. The company was founded by Will Keith Kellogg in 1906 and is
headquartered in Chicago, IL.
