Invitation Homes, Inc. engages in the acquisition, renovation, leasing, and
operation of single-family homes as rental properties, including single-family
homes in planned unit developments. Its services include property management,
selection of homes, maintenance program, and online payment. The company was
founded by Marcus Ridgway, Dallas Tanner, and Brad Greiwe in 2012 and is
headquartered in Dallas, TX.
