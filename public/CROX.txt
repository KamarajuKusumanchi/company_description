Crocs, Inc. engages in the design, development, manufacturing, worldwide
marketing, sale, and distribution of casual footwear and accessories for men,
women, and children. It operates through the following segments: North America,
Asia Pacific, Europe, Middle East, Africa, and Latin America (EMEALA), and
HEYDUDE Brand. The company was founded by Scott Seamans, George B. Boedecker,
Jr., and Lyndon V. Hanson III in 2002 and is headquartered in Broomfield, CO.
