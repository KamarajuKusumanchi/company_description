Molson Coors Beverage Company manufactures, markets, and sells beer and other
malt beverage products under various brands in the Americas, Europe, Middle
East, Africa, and Asia Pacific. It offers flavored malt beverages, craft, and
ready to drink beverages. The company was formerly known as Molson Coors
Brewing Company and changed its name to Molson Coors Beverage Company in
January 2020. Molson Coors Beverage Company was founded in 1774 and is based in
Golden, Colorado.
