The J. M. Smucker Co. engages in the manufacture and marketing of food and
beverage products. It operates through the following segments: U.S. Retail
Coffee, U.S. Retail Consumer Foods, U.S. Retail Pet Foods, and Others. The U.
S. Retail Coffee segment includes domestic sales of Folgers, Dunkin' Donuts,
and Cafe Bustelo branded coffee. The U.S. Retail Consumer Foods segment focuses
on selling Smucker's, Jif, and Crisco branded products. The U.S. Retail Pet
Foods offers Rachael Ray Nutrish, Meow Mix, Milk-Bone, Natural Balance, Kibbles
'n Bits, 9Lives, Nature's Recipe, and Pup-Peroni branded products. The company
was founded by Jerome Monroe Smucker in 1897 and is headquartered in Orrville,
OH.
