Las Vegas Sands Corp., together with its subsidiaries, develops, owns, and
operates integrated resorts in Asia and the United States. It owns and operates
The Venetian Macao Resort Hotel, the Londoner Macao, The Parisian Macao, The
Plaza Macao and Four Seasons Hotel Macao, Cotai Strip, and the Sands Macao in
Macao, the People's Republic of China; and Marina Bay Sands in Singapore. The
company also owns and operates The Venetian Resort Hotel Casino on the Las
Vegas Strip; and the Sands Expo and Convention Center in Las Vegas, Nevada. Its
integrated resorts feature accommodations, gaming, entertainment and retail
malls, convention and exhibition facilities, celebrity chef restaurants, and
other amenities. Las Vegas Sands Corp. was founded in 1988 and is based in Las
Vegas, Nevada.
