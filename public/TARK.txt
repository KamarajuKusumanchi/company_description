AXS 2X Innovation ETF seeks daily investment results, before fees and expenses,
of 200% of the daily performance of the ARK Innovation ETF. The fund will enter
into one or more swap agreements with major global financial institutions for a
specified period ranging from a day to more than one year whereby the fund and
the global financial institution will agree to exchange the return (or
differentials in rates of return) earned or realized on the ARK Innovation ETF.
It is non-diversified.
