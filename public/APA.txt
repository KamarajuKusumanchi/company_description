APA Corporation, through its subsidiaries, explores for, develops, and produces
oil and gas properties. It has operations in the United States, Egypt, and the
United Kingdom, as well as has exploration activities offshore Suriname. The
company also operates gathering, processing, and transmission assets in West
Texas, as well as holds ownership in four Permian-to-Gulf Coast pipelines. APA
Corporation was founded in 1954 and is based in Houston, Texas.
