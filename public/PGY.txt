Pagaya Technologies Ltd. operates as a financial technology company in Israel,
the United States, and the Cayman Islands. It develops and implements
proprietary artificial intelligence technology and related software solutions
to assist partners to originate loans and other assets. Its partners include
high-growth financial technology companies, incumbent financial institutions,
auto finance providers, and brokers. The company was founded in 2016 and is
headquartered in Tel Aviv, Israel.
