Burlington Stores, Inc. engages in the retail of off-price apparel and home
products. It offers women's ready-to-wear apparel, accessories, footwear,
menswear, youth apparel, baby, coats, beauty, toys, and gifts. It operates
through the following segments: Ladies Apparel, Accessories and Shoes, Home,
Mens Apparel, Kids Apparel and Baby, and Outerwear. The company was founded in
1972 and is headquartered in Burlington, NJ.
