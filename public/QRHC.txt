Quest Resource Holding Corp. engages in the provision of waste and recycling
services to customers from across multiple industry sectors that are typically
larger, multi-location businesses. It focuses on the waste streams and
recyclables from big box, food chain, and other retailers, automotive repair,
maintenance, and tire operations, truck and bus fleet operators, manufacturing
plants, multi-family and commercial properties, and construction and demolition
projects. The company was founded in July 2002 and is headquartered in The
Colony, TX.
