Super Micro Computer, Inc. engages in the distribution and manufacture of
information technology solutions and other computer products. Its products
include twin solutions, MP servers, GPU and coprocessor, MicroCloud, AMD
solutions, power supplies, SuperServer, storage, motherboards, chassis, super
workstations, accessories, SuperRack and server management products. The
company was founded by Charles Liang, Yih-Shyan Liaw, Sara Liu, and Chiu-Chu
Liu Liang in September 1993 and is headquartered in San Jose, CA.
