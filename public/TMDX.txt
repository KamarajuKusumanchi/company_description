TransMedics Group, Inc. operates as a commercial stage medical technology
company. The firm engages in the development and commercialization of organ
care system platform. It focuses on the preservation of human organs for
transplant in a near-physiologic condition to address the limitations of cold
storage organ preservation. The company was founded by Waleed H. Hassanein in
August 1998 and is headquartered in Andover, MA.
