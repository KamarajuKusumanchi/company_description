Newmont Corporation engages in the production and exploration of gold. It also
explores for copper, silver, zinc, and lead. The company has operations and/or
assets in the United States, Canada, Mexico, Dominican Republic, Peru,
Suriname, Argentina, Chile, Australia, and Ghana. As of December 31, 2021, it
had proven and probable gold reserves of 92.8 million ounces and land position
of 62,800 square kilometers. The company was founded in 1916 and is
headquartered in Denver, Colorado.
