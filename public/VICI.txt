VICI Properties is an experiential real estate investment trust that owns one
of the largest portfolios of market-leading gaming, hospitality and
entertainment destinations, including the world-renowned Caesars Palace. VICI
Properties' national, geographically diverse portfolio consists of 29 gaming
facilities comprising over 48 million square feet and features approximately
19,200 hotel rooms and more than 200 restaurants, bars and nightclubs. Its
properties are leased to industry leading gaming and hospitality operators,
including Caesars Entertainment, Inc., Century Casinos Inc., Hard Rock
International, JACK Entertainment and Penn National Gaming, Inc. VICI
Properties also owns four championship golf courses and 34 acres of undeveloped
land adjacent to the Las Vegas Strip. VICI Properties' strategy is to create
the nation's highest quality and most productive experiential real estate
portfolio.
