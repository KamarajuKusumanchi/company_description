Exxon Mobil Corporation explores for and produces crude oil and natural gas in
the United States and internationally. It operates through Upstream,
Downstream, and Chemical segments. The company is also involved in the
manufacture, trade, transport, and sale of crude oil, natural gas, petroleum
products, petrochemicals, and other specialty products; manufactures and sells
petrochemicals, including olefins, polyolefins, aromatics, and various other
petrochemicals; and captures and stores carbon, hydrogen, and biofuels. As of
December 31, 2021, it had approximately 20,528 net operated wells with proved
reserves. The company was founded in 1870 and is headquartered in Irving,
Texas.
