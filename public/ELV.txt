Elevance Health Inc. operates as a health benefits company. It supports
consumers, families, and communities across the entire care journey connecting
to the care, support, and resources to lead healthier lives. It serves
approximately 118 million people through a portfolio of medical, digital,
pharmacy, behavioral, clinical, and care solutions. The company was formerly
known as Anthem, Inc. and changed its name to Elevance Health Inc. in June
2022. Elevance Health Inc. was founded in 1944 and is headquartered in
Indianapolis, Indiana.
