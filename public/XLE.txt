Energy Select Sector SPDR Fund seeks to provide investment results that, before
expenses, correspond generally to the price and yield performance of publicly
traded equity securities of companies in the Energy Select Sector Index. In
seeking to track the performance of the index, the fund employs a replication
strategy. It generally invests substantially all, but at least 95%, of its
total assets in the securities comprising the index. The index includes
companies that have been identified as Energy companies by the GICS, including
securities of companies from the following industries: oil, gas and consumable
fuels; and energy equipment and services. It is non-diversified.
