Healthpeak Properties, Inc. is a fully integrated real estate investment trust
(REIT) and S&P 500 company. Healthpeak owns and develops high-quality real
estate in the three private-pay healthcare asset classes of Life Science,
Medical Office and Senior Housing, designed to provide stability through the
inevitable industry cycles. At Healthpeak, we pair our deep understanding of
the healthcare real estate market with a strong vision for long-term growth.
