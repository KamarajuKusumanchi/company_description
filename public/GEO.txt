The GEO Group, Inc. engages in the design, financing, development, and support
services for secure facilities, processing centers, and community re-entry
facilities. It operates through the following segments: U.S. Secure, Electronic
Monitoring and Supervision, Reentry, and International Services. The U.S.
Secure Services segment encompasses the United States-based public-private
partnership corrections and detention business. The Electronic Monitoring and
Supervision Services segment conducts its services in the U.S. The Reentry
Services Segment consists of various community-based and reentry services. The
International Services segment consists of public-private partnership secure
services operations in Australia and South Africa. The company was founded by
George C. Zoley in 1984 and is headquartered in Boca Raton, FL.
