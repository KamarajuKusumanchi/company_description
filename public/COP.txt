ConocoPhillips explores for, produces, transports, and markets crude oil,
bitumen, natural gas, liquefied natural gas (LNG), and natural gas liquids
worldwide. It primarily engages in the conventional and tight oil reservoirs,
shale gas, heavy oil, LNG, oil sands, and other production operations. The
company's portfolio includes unconventional plays in North America;
conventional assets in North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia; various LNG
developments; oil sands assets in Canada; and an inventory of conventional and
unconventional exploration prospects. ConocoPhillips was founded in 1917 and is
headquartered in Houston, Texas.
