Host Hotels & Resorts, Inc. is an S&P 500 company and is the largest lodging
real estate investment trust and one of the largest owners of luxury and upper-
upscale hotels. The Company currently owns 74 properties in the United States
and five properties internationally totaling approximately 46,100 rooms. The
Company also holds non-controlling interests in six domestic and one
international joint ventures. Guided by a disciplined approach to capital
allocation and aggressive asset management, the Company partners with premium
brands such as Marriott, Ritz-Carlton, Westin, Sheraton, W, St. Regis, The
Luxury Collection, Hyatt, Fairmont, Hilton, Swissotel, ibis and Novotel, as
well as independent brands. For additional information, please visit the
Company's website at www.hosthotels.com.
