Zillow Group, Inc., a digital real estate company, operates real estate brands
on mobile applications and Websites in the United States. The company operates
through three segments: Homes; Internet, Media & Technology; and Mortgages. The
Homes segment is involved in resale of homes; and title and escrow services to
home buyers and sellers, including title search procedures for title insurance
policies, escrow, and other closing services. The IMT segment offers premier
agent, rentals, and new construction marketplaces, as well as dotloop, display,
and other advertising, as well as business software solutions. The Mortgage
segment provides home loans; and marketing products including custom quote and
connect services. Its portfolio of brands includes Zillow Rentals, Trulia,
StreetEasy, Zillow Closing Services, HotPads, and Out East. The company was
incorporated in 2004 and is headquartered in Seattle, Washington.
