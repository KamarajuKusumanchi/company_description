UTZ Brands, Inc. manufactures, markets, and distributes branded snacking
products. It offers a broad range of salty snacks, including potato chips,
pretzels, cheese, veggie, other snacks and pork skins. The firm's brands
include Utz, Zapp's, Golden Flake, Good Health, Boulder Canyon and Hawaiian.
The company was founded in 1921 and is headquartered in Hanover, PA.
