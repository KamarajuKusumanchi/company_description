The Home Depot, Inc. engages in the sale of building materials and home
improvement products. Its products include building materials, home improvement
products, lawn and garden products and decor products. The firm operates
through the following geographical segments: U.S., Canada and Mexico. It offers
home improvement installation services, and tool and equipment rental. The
company was founded by Bernard Marcus, Arthur M. Blank, Kenneth Gerald Langone
and Pat Farrah on June 29, 1978, and is headquartered at Atlanta, GA.
