MicroSectors FANG+ Index 3X Leveraged ETN seeks return linked to a three times
leveraged participation in the daily performance of the NYSE FANG+ Index, total
return (the index). The notes are intended to be daily trading tools for
sophisticated investors to manage daily trading risks as part of an overall
diversified portfolio. The index is an equal-dollar weighted index designed to
represent a segment of the technology and consumer discretionary sectors
consisting of highly-traded growth stocks of technology and tech-enabled
companies.
